About Me

Welcome to my corner of the music world.

I’m Wiley Scott, a musician deeply influenced by nu-metal bands such as Slipknot, Mudvayne, and Korn.
These influences have shaped my own sound, blending dark, electric, and experimental elements to create something unique.

Music has been my passion for years, and I’m proud to say that I’ve just released my second album.
Each track is a story to be told, exploring themes that I resonate with.

Thank you for being here and supporting my music.
Your presence means everything as I continue to follow my passion.
